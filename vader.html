Darth Vader Simplification!

When you simplify something you want to have as little as possible but still keeping the characteristic. Vader is such an iconic character so you don't need to keep many details to still be recognizable. For me is his mouth the most characteristic thing on his helmet, so I started with just keeping that. It was great and you could actually recognize him with just the mouth. But I decided to add some structure from his helmet aswell because it looked way more epic and was still simplified a lot.
